In addition to his cricketing achievements, Kohli is known for his dedication to fitness and his involvement in charitable causes. He is also respected for his humility and sportsmanship, and has won awards for his fair play. Kohli's talent and leadership have helped guide the Indian team to victories in numerous tournaments and series, and he is a key figure in the world of cricket.
